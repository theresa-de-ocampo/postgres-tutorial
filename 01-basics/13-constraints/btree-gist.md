# `btree_gist`

Upon its installation, it creates the following functions (total of 470), and data types.

## [Functions](https://stackoverflow.com/questions/1347282/how-can-i-get-a-list-of-all-functions-stored-in-the-database-of-a-particular-sch)

```sql
SELECT routines.routine_name, parameters.data_type, parameters.ordinal_position
FROM information_schema.routines
    LEFT JOIN information_schema.parameters ON routines.specific_name=parameters.specific_name
WHERE routines.specific_schema='public'
ORDER BY routines.routine_name, parameters.ordinal_position;
```
